Portfolio

CoinJar Touch

CoinJar is a simple everyday bitcoin payments app. My involvement was finishing the product after the initial framework and basic features were put in place. As the sole Android developer on the project at the time, my responsibilities included delivering the app on tight deadlines as well as consulting on the appropriate UX practices.

  • Who? CoinJar
  • When? 2014 – 2015
  • Where? Google Play

SafeMate

SafeMate is an app designed to help first responders with identifying the rescuee by scanning their NFC-enabled wristband and providing their medical records. The second version of the app that I delivered was a complete rewrite, which, in addition to containing new features, also introduced modern best practices and platform-recommended tooling to the project.

  • Who? Safe Mate Australia
  • When? 2015
  • Where? Google Play

CarsGuide

CarsGuide.com.au is an online service for selling new and used cars. Working in a team of two (part of Vivant), the app was delivered from scratch in a short amount of time and following the material design guidelines, dramatically improved the user rating for the client.

  • Who? Vivant
  • When? 2015
  • Where? Google Play

Foxtel Guide

Foxtel Guide is the TV schedule app for Foxtel subscribers designed for checking detailed information about the programming, setting reminders and remote recording while away from the set-top box. Working with the original author of the Android app and teams of varying sizes over the 3 years, my function varied from bug fixes to leading the whole team through a major redesign.

  • Who? Neon Stingray (Valtech Australia)
  • When? 2011 – 2013
  • Where? Google Play

Songl

Songl was shut down in 2014.

  • Who? Neon Stingray (Valtech Australia)
  • When? 2012 – 2013
  • Where? No longer available :(

  • When? 2013 – 2014
